Kazi Nazrul Islam

The National Poet of Bangladesh

Rebel Poet

Kazi Nazrul Islam is the national poet of Bangladesh who is especially recognized as a Bidrohi kobi(rebel poet) around the country.Nazrul is famous poet, musician and philosopher of Bangladesh.The famous poet Nazrul's writtings deals with the revolution of that period of the Biritish hovernment.His revolutionary attempt helped him to be a national poet of Bangladesh.He has raised his voice againt any kinds of facism and oppretion and got popularity swiftly around the Indian suncontinent.

Awesome Writer

Nazul's writings explored themes such as love,freedom,humanity and revolution. He apposed all forms of bigotry and fundamentalism,including relegious,caste-based and gender-based.Throughout his career,Nazrul wrote short stories,novels,and essays but is best known for his songs and poems.He pioneered new music forms such as Bengali ghazals.Nazrul wrote and composed music for nearly 4000 songs(many recorded on HMV and gramophone records),collectively known as Nazrul Geeti.

"Love has no meaning or amount"-Kazi Nazrul Islam
